Resources

Throughout the years I have read quite the few cook books. Professional Cooking By Wayne Gisslen is the ultimate 'learn to cook' book. It has tons of valuable information. It will show you butchery techniques, cooking methods, how and when to use certain ingredients, and recipes to reinforce what you've learned throughout the book. As for butchery, in Mastering the Art of Poultry, Meat, and Game, you learn many different ways to use every piece of your meat so that nothing goes to waste. Below are a couple included videos of some culinary basics. Learning the 5 Mothe Sauces and learning how to simply Roast a Chicken are tools that you will use time and time again.

Professional Cooking By: Wayne Gisslen

This is the holy grail of cook books. For the beginner home chef who want so learn more about the fundamentals of cooking, this is the book for you. You will learn all about the Mother Sauces and extensively about the most popular cooking techniques. This resource will also teach you basic butchery and has an impressive list of recipes for you to try out your newly learned techniques!

Mastering the Art of Poultry, Meat, and Game
By Anneka Manning

A practical yet beautiful, modern cookbook with everything you need to know about preparing, cooking and serving varieties of meat. Perfect recipes for a summer cookout to impress your friends, or to woo a special someone with a chateaubriand. Learning basic butchery will help you save cash at home as you will be able to utilize everything with less waste!

"Plenty More Bu Yotam Ottolenghi"

Featuring more than 150 vegetarian dishes organized by cooking method, this book will give you budget friendly and captivating recipes that will not only be pleasing to the tastebuds, but also the eyes. It explores the diverse realm of vegetarian food with a wholly original approach. Most dishes can be used as a side or as an entire meal itself!

Learning the 5 Mother Sauces

Learn how to make the 5 Mother Sauces from scratch! You will learn how to make Bechamel, Tomato, Hollandaise, Veloute, and Espagnole Sauce. These 5 classical sauces are the basis of almost all classical french cuisine. They may sound fancy, but they are easy and cheap to make!

How-To Roast a Whole Chicken

One of the main meals that the home cook should know, is how to roast a whole chicken. Buying a whole chicken in most cases is much less expensive than purchasing the pieces individually. Learning this skill will fill your home and stomachs with delicious food!
