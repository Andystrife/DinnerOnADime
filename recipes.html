Recipes

Looking for a couple new ideas to utilize in the kitchen? Something that is easy on the old coin purse while still being healthy and nutritious? Look no further! I have done the work for you and provided a couple of new and fresh solutions to the age old question, "What should I cook for dinner tonight?". We have a variety of recipes for you to follow from different parts of the world! From Indian to American, and Italian to latin, we've got your flavors picked out and ready to go. And if you still have leftovers, try out the "What's In the Fridge Fried Rice"!
Bon Appetit!

Loaded Potato Frittata

This Recipes comes from 5 Dollar Dinners and can be used for Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner! It is a very versitile dish that you can make your own additions to! Want mushrooms in there? Go ahead and add mushrooms! The sky is the limit with this recipe!

Easy Tuna Noodle Casserole

Looking for a classic easy to make dinner? Look no further with this Easy Tuna Noodle Casserole. It can be prepared in less than an hour and the kids will love it! It features many ingredients that you will typically already have in your cupboard too!

"What's In the Fridge Fried Rice"

This recipe is great because you can use whatever you have left in your frdge to add to the rice. Got some chicken? Throw it in! Got some celery? Throw it in! Not too sure what to do with those leftover boiled carrots? Chop them up and throw them in!

Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala

Enjoy this recipe for an aromatic exotic flavor. Chicken and curry and coconut milk or cream is a match made in heaven and this recipe will take you all the way to flavor country. And sir, it is a big country.

Tomato Tortellini Soup

Try this ultimate comfort meal for the fam jam! This is a beautiful classic for those fall and winter days. Warm up with some hearty tortellini and rich tomato broth. This will definitely be one of your "go-to's" in the colder seasons!

Bean and Cheese Burritos

Take taco Tuesday to the next level with the make ahead bean and cheese burritos. Use your imagination and add your own special ingredients too! Pick up a bottle of hot sauce and some sour cream and you got a winning flavor combination!

Roasted Veggies and Chicken

Want something inexpensive yet still very healthy? Check out this riff on a classic family meal. You've got roasted veggies and roasted chicken. What more could you want? Nice and healthy as well as nice and healthy on the wallet!
