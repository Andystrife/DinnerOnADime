Techniques

The Culinary Arts is a learned skill, and as they say in the hit Disney movie Ratatoulli, "Anyone Can Cook!". It's true as long as you take the time to learn the skills and techniques. I have included here a couple essential techniques that every home cook should learn. Knowing how to poach, stew, sear, and broil are techniques that will bring your cooking to the next level! Knife skills are also just as important as knowing how to cook your meal. We take focus on safety and precision. It will be a lot harder to to truss a chicken with only 9 fingers!

Basic Knife Skills

Learning to use a knife properly is one of the most important and safest things you can do while working in the kitchen. Be sure to have the right knives for the job and always remember, a dull knife is a dangerous knife! Keep them sharp!
