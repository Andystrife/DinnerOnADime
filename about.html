Chef Andy Dinsmore

Chef Andy graduated from George Brown College with Honors from their Chef Training Program in 2012. From there he was hired into a line cook position in the iconic Distillery District. After 2 years of honing his skills as a line cook, he accepted a Kitchen Supervisor roll in downtown Oakville. Here he extensively learn the value of good spice combinations and new cooking techniques. He was later offered a Head Chef position also located in Oakville where he stayed for 7 years. Chef Andy is now married with a 1 year old son. He spends his time experimenting with new cooking concepts and techniques and teaches close friends and family the value of cooking.
